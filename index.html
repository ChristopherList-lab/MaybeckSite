About

We build AI tools for production teams. We know your workflows because we've run them.

Maybeck builds software that handles the operational side of live production. Troubleshooting signal chains, coordinating crews, managing schedules, generating content from your work. Every tool is designed around how production teams operate in the field.

Our founder spent two decades running AVL rigs and two decades building AI systems at scale. Maybeck exists because production teams deserve tools built by people who've done the job.
