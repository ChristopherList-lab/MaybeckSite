Post Genius - AI Content Generation for Production Companies

AI-powered content generation for production companies. Turn your work into social proof that books the next gig.

Mission

Mission

Production companies create incredible work but rarely have the bandwidth to market it. Post Genius solves that gap by turning raw production assets into polished social content.

Feed it photos, video clips, or show notes from a recent event. Post Genius generates platform-optimized captions, hashtag strategies, and case study drafts.

Key Capabilities

Capabilities

image

Content from Assets

Upload event photos or clips. Post Genius generates ready-to-publish social posts with captions and platform formatting.

edit_note

Case Study Drafts

Transform event recaps into client-facing case studies. Highlight technical challenges, solutions, and outcomes.

monitoring

Performance Insights

Track which content drives engagement and inquiries. Data-driven recommendations for your next post.
