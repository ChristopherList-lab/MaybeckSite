AVL Copilot - AI Assistant for Audio Video Lighting Professionals

An AI copilot built for audio, video, and lighting professionals. Your production knowledge base, always on call.

Mission

Mission

AVL Copilot brings the depth of a seasoned production veteran into a conversational AI interface. It understands the real workflows, gear, and terminology that audio, video, and lighting professionals use every day.

Built from the ground up with industry-specific training data, AVL Copilot can troubleshoot signal chains, recommend equipment configurations, generate show files, and assist with system design.
